Our Story

Mustang Kyidug USA is a nonprofit organization established to support and unite the Mustangi community living in the United States while preserving the rich cultural heritage of Mustang, Nepal. Rooted in the values of community, tradition, and development, we strive to create a platform where Mustangi people can come together, celebrate their identity, and contribute to the well-being of both their homeland and their community abroad.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission is to preserve, promote, and protect the cultural heritage of Mustang while fostering unity and mutual support among Mustangi individuals living in the United States. We aim to:

  • Strengthen cultural identity by organizing events, festivals, and educational programs.
  • Support Mustangi individuals by offering guidance, networking, and community support.
  • Contribute to the development of Mustang, Nepal, through charitable projects and social initiatives.

Vision

We envision a strong, united Mustangi community that upholds and celebrates its unique heritage while adapting to the modern world. Our goal is to ensure that the values, traditions, and identity of Mustang are passed on to future generations.

What We Do

Cultural Promotion & Events

Mustang Kyidug USA plays a key role in preserving Mustangi traditions through various cultural programs. We organize annual celebrations for important festivals such as Tiji, Losar (Tibetan New Year), and Chhongu, as well as community gatherings where Mustangi dance, music, and customs are showcased.

Community Assistance & Networking

We provide a platform for Mustangi individuals in the USA to connect, support one another, and share opportunities. Whether it’s assisting new immigrants, providing educational resources, or facilitating professional networking, we aim to strengthen the bonds within our community.

Development Initiatives for Mustang

Mustang Kyidug USA is dedicated to supporting development projects in Mustang, Nepal. Our initiatives include:

  • Educational programs: Scholarships and school improvement projects.
  • Healthcare initiatives: Medical aid and support for rural health centers.
  • Infrastructure projects: Community development and preservation of historical sites.

Join Us

Mustang Kyidug USA welcomes everyone who shares our passion for preserving Mustang’s heritage and supporting its people. Whether you are from Mustang, a second-generation Mustangi, or someone passionate about Himalayan culture, you can get involved in our mission.
