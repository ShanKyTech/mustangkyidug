About Us

Mustang Kyidug USA is a nonprofit organization dedicated to preserving the rich culture of the Mustang community in Nepal. Our mission is to promote, protect, and preserve our unique traditions while supporting our local communities both in Nepal and abroad.

Established by passionate individuals, our organization serves as a bridge between generations, ensuring that our traditions, values, and identity remain strong, even as we integrate into modern society.

Our Mission

  • To preserve and promote Mustangi culture, language, and traditions.
  • To support Mustangi individuals in the USA through education, networking, and community assistance.
  • To contribute to the development of Mustang, Nepal, through charitable initiatives, infrastructure projects, and education programs.

What We Do

Cultural Preservation & Events

Mustang Kyidug USA organizes various cultural events, festivals, and educational programs to keep our heritage alive. From celebrating Tiji Festival to hosting traditional dance and music performances, we ensure that our customs continue to thrive.

Community Support & Networking

We assist Mustangi individuals and families in adjusting to life in the USA by offering resources, networking opportunities, and community gatherings. Whether it's helping newcomers settle in or connecting professionals within our community, we strive to create a strong support system.

Development Initiatives for Mustang, Nepal

We actively participate in social welfare programs in Mustang, supporting projects related to education, healthcare, and infrastructure development. Our goal is to give back to our homeland and improve the lives of those in the region.

Join Us

We welcome all members of the Mustangi community and those who share our love for this unique culture. Whether you want to participate in events, volunteer, or contribute to our projects, Mustang Kyidug USA provides various ways to get involved.

Stay connected with us and be a part of a growing movement that cherishes tradition while embracing the future!
